Attire

Most people will dress fancy or semi-fancy. We recommend bringing a few layers—February in Florida can be as low as 40° or as high as 75°.

Hotels

The Courtyard Orlando Downtown is the best mix of proximity to the venue, price, and quality. We also love Airbnb.

Uber Transportation

Orlando’s buses & taxis aren’t the best, so Uber is ideal if you don’t want to rent a car (or plan to have a drink or two). If you enter the code FEELING22 into the app you get your first ride free.

Venue

Both the ceremony and reception will be at The Acre (4421 Edgewater Dr.). Arrive at 4:00p on Saturday, Feb 20!
